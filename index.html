Free Workshops Upcoming Events

Never made a zine before? We will teach you!

We will teach you to fold an 8 page booklet (or mini comic) from a single sheet of paper. Collage supplies will be provided. You are welcome to bring your own active projects or art supplies.

Come make zines, share your favorite zines, or just hang out with fellow zinesters.

About

Bremerton Zine Fest is Kitsap County's first and currently only annual convention for buying and trading zines! Started by local Bremertonian Dani Gray in 2023, Bremerton Zine Fest celebrates local artists and DIY creativity.

Dani Gray

Founder and Organizer of Bremerton Zine Fest
